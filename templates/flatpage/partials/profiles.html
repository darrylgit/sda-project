Daniel Ginsberg

Co-Founder & CEO

BA’ Sustainable Business & Sociology '17

Daniel has over 5 years of coaching experience.

As a coach, Daniel has been on the scene since high school, spending his last summer both preparing for college ball and assisting one of the top AAU basketball programs in Virginia. He spent the next four years training other athletes during his off season and offered the same regimen when he took his talents overseas.

Daniel holds a Plant-Based Nutrition Certification from Cornell University and is a champion of health, educating and implementing dieting strategies for athletes.

Sampson Dale

Co-Founder & Head Director

BA’ Communications 17'
MA’ Athletic Coaching Education 19'

Sam is a seasoned professional with over 10 years of training and development experience.

A mentor by nature, Sam effectively projects leadership, passion and self-discipline through his coaching. He is well versed in strategic, functional basketball training and integrates innovative training ideologies such as block and randomized practice into SDA workouts; coupled with his own experience as a former athlete, he brings a sense of knowledge and structure to every session.

Sam is currently the assistant women’s basketball coach at Green Mountain College.
